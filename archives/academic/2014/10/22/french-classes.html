GSAN offers free French classes for members of the graduate student neuroscience community. Classes are held at the MNI and run throughout the academic year. Three different levels are currently offered: Beginner’s level: Tuesdays 6-7pm Intermediate level: Thursdays 6-7pm Advanced level: Wednesdays 6-7pm

For questions or if you would like to register, please contact our French classe coordinator Anne at: anne.loffler@mail.mcgill.ca
