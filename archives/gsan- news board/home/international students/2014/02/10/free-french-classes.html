All IPN students are welcome to join free French Classes organized by the GSAN and moderated by IPN students. Classes will start by the end en February-first week of March and will take place once a week at 6:30 pm. Location T.B.A.

Beginners: Classes will alternate between Thursdays and Mondays. First week Thursday, next week Monday, third week Thursday again etc. Basic-Intermediate: Classes will alternate between Friday and Tuesdays. Advanced: Classes will take place on Wednesdays. A link to register will be posted next week!
