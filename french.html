Free French classes for members

GSAN offers free French classes for its members. These classes are taught by student volunteers from the IPN and run once a week during the fall and winter semester. Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced level courses are offered. All three levels cover grammar, vocabulary, speaking, and comprehension.
For more information or to register for French classes, please contact the 2015-2016 GSAN French Class Coordinator, Maryse Thomas, at gsan.french@gmail.com
